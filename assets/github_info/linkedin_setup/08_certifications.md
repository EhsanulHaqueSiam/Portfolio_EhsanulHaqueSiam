## üí° Certification Tips

### Quick Wins
1. Get HackerRank badges (1-2 hours each)
2. Complete Google's ML Crash Course (free)
3. Take LinkedIn Skill Assessments
4. Get freeCodeCamp responsive design cert

### Prioritize
- Focus on certifications matching your target role
- AI/ML roles ‚Üí Google ML, TensorFlow
- Android roles ‚Üí Meta Android cert
- Backend roles ‚Üí AWS/cloud certs

### Don't Over-certify
- Quality > quantity
- 3-5 relevant certifications is enough
- Old/expired certifications can be removed
