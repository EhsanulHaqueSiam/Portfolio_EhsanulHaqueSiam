- Most important/impressive item first
- Website/portfolio should typically be #1
- Research is impressive - keep it high

- **External links** - websites, papers, videos
- **LinkedIn articles** - if you write on LinkedIn
- **LinkedIn posts** - your best-performing posts
- **Documents** - PDFs, presentations

### Updating
- Change Featured items regularly
- Showcase recent work
- Remove outdated projects
