## üìä Recommendation Strategy

### Goal: Get 3-5 recommendations

**Phase 1 (Week 1):**
1. Identify 8-10 potential recommenders
2. Send personalized requests to top 5
3. Offer to write recommendations in return

**Phase 2 (Week 2):**
1. Follow up with non-responders
2. Send requests to next 3-5 people
3. Write recommendations for those who requested

**Phase 3 (Ongoing):**
1. Thank everyone who wrote recommendations
2. Write thoughtful recommendations in return
3. Request new recommendations after major projects

---

## üîÑ Writing Recommendations for Others

Always offer to write recommendations in return. Tips:

1. Be specific about projects you worked on together
2. Include metrics when possible
3. Mention both technical and soft skills
4. Keep it 3-5 sentences (150-300 words)
5. Proofread before sending

---

## üí° Recommendation Tips

### Timing
- Request after successful project completion
- Avoid exam/busy periods
- Give 1-2 weeks for response

### Making it Easy
- Provide specific points they can mention
- Share your LinkedIn profile link
- Offer to write their recommendation first

### Following Up
- Send gentle reminder after 1 week
- Don't be pushy - some people are busy
- Thank them publicly when posted
