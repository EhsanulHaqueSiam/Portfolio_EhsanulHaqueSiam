## ğŸ“ How to Add Publications

1. Go to your LinkedIn profile
2. Click "Add profile section"
3. Under "Additional" select "Add publications"
4. Fill in all fields

### Fields to Complete:
- **Title:** Full paper title
- **Publication/Publisher:** Journal/Conference name
- **Publication Date:** Month and Year
- **Publication URL:** Link to paper (if available)
- **Description:** Abstract or summary
- **Authors:** You and co-authors (add their LinkedIn profiles if they have one)

---

## ğŸŒ Where to Host Your Paper

If your paper isn't yet online, consider:

1. **ResearchGate** - Academic social network
   - Create profile, upload paper
   - Get DOI for preprints
   
2. **Academia.edu** - Academic sharing platform
   - Free to upload
   - Good for visibility
   
3. **arXiv** - Preprint server (for CS/AI papers)
   - Open access
   - Widely respected
   
4. **Google Scholar** - Will index once published
   - Create author profile
   - Track citations
