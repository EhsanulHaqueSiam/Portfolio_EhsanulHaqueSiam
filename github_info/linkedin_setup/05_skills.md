## üí° Skills Section Tips

### Don't Add Skills You Can't Discuss
- Only add skills you can speak about in an interview
- Be prepared to explain your experience level

### Skill Levels (in your head)
- **Expert:** 2+ years, production experience
- **Advanced:** 1+ years, multiple projects
- **Intermediate:** Several projects, ongoing learning
- **Beginner:** Just started, 1-2 projects

### Update Regularly
- Add new skills as you learn them
- Remove outdated or irrelevant skills
