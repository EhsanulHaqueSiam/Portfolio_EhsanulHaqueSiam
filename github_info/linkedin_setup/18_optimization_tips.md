## üéØ LinkedIn SEO (Search Engine Optimization)

### How LinkedIn Search Works
Recruiters search for keywords. Your profile ranks higher when:
1. Keywords appear in **headline, about, experience**
2. Profile is **complete** (All-Star level)
3. You have **recent activity**
4. You have **connections** in that field

### Keywords to Include Everywhere

```
Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Deep Learning

```

**Database/Backend Keywords:**

## üí° Pro Recruiter Tips

### What Recruiters Search For

Their search filters:
- Job title keywords
- Skills
- Location
- Years of experience
- Current company
- Education

### How to Rank Higher

1. **Match job titles exactly** - Use "Machine Learning Engineer" not just "ML Dev"
2. **List skills from job postings**
3. **Update regularly** - Active profiles rank higher
4. **Get endorsements** - Social proof matters
5. **Complete profile** - All-Star status required

### Recruiter Psychology

They spend 6 seconds scanning:
1. Photo (professional?)
2. Headline (relevant?)
3. Current role (experience?)
4. Education (qualified?)
5. Skills (match job?)

Make these count!
