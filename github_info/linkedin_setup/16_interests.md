## üí° Interest Tips

### Strategic Following
- Follow companies where you want to work
- Their job posts will appear in your feed
- You'll be suggested to their employees

### Engagement
- Like and comment on company posts
- Share insights (builds thought leadership)
- Engage with posts from target companies

### Don't Overdo It
- Focus on quality, not quantity
- Follow relevant to your career goals
- Unfollow irrelevant pages
